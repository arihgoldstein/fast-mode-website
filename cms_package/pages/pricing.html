Questions, answered

What do I get on the Free plan?

You can host a full site on a Fastmode subdomain, publish CMS content, edit page copy visually, and manage SEO — all for $0.

Can I connect my own domain?

Yep — that's included on Pro. Point your DNS, and we handle hosting + CDN delivery.

What happens when I upgrade?

Your existing site stays exactly the same. We remove branding and switch you to custom domain + CDN.

Can I downgrade later?

Totally. You can downgrade anytime — we'll keep your site live on a Fastmode subdomain.

Do you support tracking pixels and scripts?

Yes. Add anything you need once, and we inject it into the head of every page.

Is the CMS included in both plans?

Yes — Blogs, Authors, Team Members, and Downloads are included in every plan.
